Who do you want to become?

 

Be the best version of yourself!

Your Private EyeStylist is more than just a personal shopper. I will give you the fashion advice to feel more confident and attractive to yourself and others. I have the technological background and experience to help you reduce digital eye strain and see with the most vivid lenses for your Rx. I dig into the latest health tools to protect your eyes for the long haul.

Whether you are unsure of what frame to select or you are looking for the right luxury and boutique eyewear to create your unique visual signature, Your Private Eyestylist will give you the same direction we offer celebrities at affordable price points. Simply select which package best fits your needs and let’s reinvent your look!

A highly individual and personalized service to guide people with their fashion and clinical/lens/health choices whether their buying online, chains or boutiques.

Eric Jay - Your Private EyeStylist

Everyday I strive to add beauty, confidence and energy to people’s faces. After all, the face is the first thing we see, far above your shoes. Choosing the right look will lead you in the direction of your goals. And there are so many choices from online take home kits to luxury eyewear. Family and friends are usually not the best beacons to turn to, nor is the self. A transformation requires unbiased and professional coaching. Much like a haircut, it’s not to be handled by whoever is in the room. Up your game to be and see a better version of yourself!

When making suggestions I consider the following:

  • How to flatter your face shape
  • Bone structure
  • Skin tone
  • Eye color
  • Tribe identity vs individual stamp
  • How to blend in and stand out
  • Type of Rx
  • Personality
  • Passion
  • Industry
  • Personal goals

For every session you book, a portion goes to a charity to help provide eyewear to those without the means to see properly

How I work

With ever increasing online eyewear purchases selecting the right look can be overwhelming and confusing. Simply upload an image of yourself, your Rx if you have one and I’ll show you what to wear and why. You may resist at first, but I’ll show you how to “see” yourself as the person you are evolving towards. Let me nudge you that right direction. Be a better version of you!
